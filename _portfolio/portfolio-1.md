The project is about checking the air quality around places in my city.  

The tools that I used are plastic, vaseline, and computers to track and analyze my data.  

Something I learned is that Air quality worsens as you reach places with fewer or no filters.  

Challenges that I faced and solved were how to track data; I solved this by using Google Slides and creating a graph.
